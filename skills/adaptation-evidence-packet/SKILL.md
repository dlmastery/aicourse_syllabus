# Adaptation Evidence Packet

A reusable **harness skill** from the *Modern AI Mastery (June 2026)* program — a small machine for repeatable thinking that turns a one-off task into a checklist that leaves behind evidence.

## Definition

- **Purpose:** assemble decision → data card → training runs → regression matrix → serving SLA into one reviewable bundle.
- **Inputs:** the capstone artifacts + all weekly evidence.
- **Required outputs:** a packet where each design decision links to the measurement that justified it, including what was tried and *cut*.
- **Evidence artifact:** the packet itself.

## Used in
- Subject 02 · Part C — Fine-Tuning & Model Adaptation · Week 10 — Capstone: A Fine-Tuned Model That Provably Beats Prompting

## How to invoke

In a Codex-style environment: `Use $adaptation-evidence-packet to ...`. Otherwise follow the Definition above as a prompt scaffold / checklist. Always end by committing the named evidence artifact.
